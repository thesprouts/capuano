sprout + Capuano

force, motion, and science standards

what we can do

one hour

  • We plan a project, collect materials, and come in for a single 1-2 hour long workshop conducted with your assistance during regular class time.

ten hours

  • We plan a longer-term project with your help, coming in for 1-2 hours each day to build on the same things, coming up with materials to complement projects and populate interest centers.

culmination

  • We come in for a week, like the previous, but also plan a larger-scale culminating work-product or event to show off our work, invite families to participate, or to share with the school community.

paint
pendula

one hour

  • set up a few pendula
  • create collaborative paintings
  • talk about gravity, weight, falling

ten hours

  • build pendula at different scales
  • vary ink materials each day
  • try different types of "dripping" painting

culmination

  • install large-scale pendulum
  • paint outdoors with chalk-based ink
  • high quality pictures, video

marker
tops

one hour

  • build marker tops with cardboard
  • test them out and make personal art
  • talk about spinning motion

ten hours

  • spin tops, design own with cardboard
  • vary shape and sizes of cardboard
  • try different types of "spinning" art

culmination

  • build a record player drawing machine
  • create and frame large-scale artworks
  • high quality pictures, video

simple
automata

one hour

  • design and draw characters and sets
  • observe differences between bases
  • talk about spinning, pushing, pulling

ten hours

  • build bases using modular pieces
  • design characters based on gestures
  • talk about converting forces

culmination

  • video record each automata in action
  • create collaborative music video
  • display gallery of wooden automata
